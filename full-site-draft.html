Our Seven Pillars

The BORDERS acronym represents the comprehensive, holistic approach we take to ministry. Some pillars represent our current active labor on the field, while others represent our deep prayer and vision for the future of this ministry.

Delivering the written Word of God to unreached and restricted people groups in their native languages.
